Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last Updated: June 30, 2025

We value your trust and are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy and Terms of Use outlines how we collect, use, protect, and manage the personal information you provide through our website. By using our site and submitting your information, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Who We Are

This website is a simple contact platform designed to allow users to send their email addresses so that we may respond to inquiries or continue communication as requested.

We do not operate as a commercial service, and we do not sell any products or collect extensive user data. Our only purpose is to facilitate contact through a secure and respectful process.

2. Information We Collect

The only personal data we collect is:

  • Email Address: Submitted by you through the contact form available on our site.

We do not ask for or collect your name, phone number, address, or any other personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used exclusively for the purpose of:

  • Responding to your message or inquiry.
  • Sending follow-up information, if appropriate, based on your inquiry.

We do not use your email for promotional purposes, mailing lists, or unsolicited communications.

4. Third-Party Service Provider

To handle form submissions, we use a third-party form service:

  • Formspree ( https://formspree.io ):
    • When you submit the form, your email is securely sent and processed by Formspree.
    • Formspree stores and manages form submissions on our behalf.
    • You can read their full Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data.

We chose Formspree because of its commitment to secure and privacy-conscious data handling.

5. Data Retention and Storage

We retain your submitted email address only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. We do not maintain databases or long-term user profiles.

All data submitted through the form is stored securely by Formspree. We do not store your data on our own servers.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we have received from you.
  • Request deletion of your data from our communication history.
  • Withdraw your consent for us to contact you at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, simply reply to our response email or submit a new message through the contact form with your request clearly stated.

7. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, or any form of tracking technologies.

We do not collect or analyze your browsing behavior, device data, or IP address.

8. Security Measures

We are committed to protecting your information. Our website uses:

  • HTTPS encryption to ensure secure data transmission.
  • Formspree's secure API to handle form submissions.
  • No client-side storage (like localStorage or cookies) for personal data.

While no system is 100% secure, we take appropriate steps to ensure the confidentiality of your data.

9. Age Limitation

This website is not intended for children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the contact form or submit any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. When we do, the "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us through the same form on the homepage.
